## Key intent
- Premium, editorial transit look with tonal layering and asymmetric spacing.
- No heavy borders/dividers for hierarchy; use surface shifts + whitespace.
- Primary CTAs use a green gradient (`primary` to `primary_container`).
- Inter typography with strong contrast and breathing room.
- Material depth through ambient shadows and layered surfaces.
